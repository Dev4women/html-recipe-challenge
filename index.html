Chicken Tagine

An easy and flavorful Moroccan dish, perfect for any meal. This slow-cooked chicken tagine is packed with aromatic spices and tender vegetables.

Preparation Time

  • Total time: 1 hour 20 minutes
  • Preparation time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking time: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 potatoes, cubed
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a tagine or large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced onions and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the garlic and spices (cumin, turmeric, paprika), stir for 1 minute.
  4. Add the chicken pieces and brown on all sides, about 8 minutes.
  5. Add the carrots and potatoes around the chicken.
  6. Pour in 1 cup of water, cover and cook on low heat for 45 minutes.
  7. Check seasoning, adjust salt and pepper.
  8. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with bread or couscous.
